The Masters of the Wrench are the masters of everything that moves. Anything mechanical, at any scale — construction cranes, windmills, watermills, drilling rigs, pumps, lift mechanisms, lock gates, carts, wagons, and the intricate clockwork inside them — falls to the Wrench. Where the Hammer builds the body of the Empire, the Wrench builds its moving parts. They are more experimental than their sister guild by temperament, since dynamic mechanisms fail in more interesting ways than walls do, and the two guilds' standing joke is that the Hammer builds the thing and the Wrench makes it regret moving.

See [[lords-of-the-hammer]] for the broader political weight the two guilds carry together.
