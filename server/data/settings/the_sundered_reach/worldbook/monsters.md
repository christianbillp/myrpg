The Spread filled this world with creatures human magic should never have brought into it, and the Sundering opened the door wider. The wilds between holdfasts are not empty. Monster factions are rarely organised in any human sense — they are warbands, packs, lairs, broods, or single ancient things with territory — but they behave with enough consistency that the local population names them and tells stories about them. Each is its own faction, with its own logic, and most are simply trying to live in a world that has too much magic in it. A few are something else.

Monsters are a **type** of minor faction, not a single unified power. Specific lairs, broods, and ancient things are for the GM to invent as the campaign requires.
