Arcania is the largest kingdom in the world and the heart of the Empire. The seat of imperial government sits within it, and so do the two institutions that matter most to anyone living under imperial law — the White Capes and the Bureau of Adjustment. To say someone answers to Arcania is, in most of the Reach, simply another way of saying they answer to the Empire.

Arcania is the senior partner in the ruling compact between the three great peoples. The dwarven kingdom and the halfling Powers hold seats beside it, but Arcania holds the throne and sets the direction. Where the dwarves bring infrastructure and the halflings bring credit, Arcania brings magic, laws, and armies.
